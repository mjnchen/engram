# Phi-3 (Microsoft)

## What it is

Small but capable open-weight model series from Microsoft Research trained on high-quality "textbook-quality" synthetic and curated data, available in 3.8B, 7B, and 14B sizes.

## What problem it solves

Small models traditionally underperform on reasoning tasks; Phi-3 demonstrates that data quality can compensate for parameter count, achieving results competitive with much larger models.
