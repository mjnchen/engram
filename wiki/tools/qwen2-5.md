# Qwen2.5

## What it is

Open-weight LLM series from Alibaba's Qwen Team with strong multilingual, math, and coding performance, available in sizes from 0.5B to 72B.

## What problem it solves

Provides a competitive open-weight alternative to GPT-4-class models with particular strength in Chinese, math reasoning, and code generation.
