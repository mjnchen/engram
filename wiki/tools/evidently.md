# Evidently

## What it is

Open-source ML and LLM monitoring framework from Evidently AI for evaluating data drift, model quality, and LLM outputs.

## What problem it solves

Production ML and LLM systems degrade silently; Evidently generates reports and monitors to detect drift and quality degradation before users notice.
