# Chatbot Arena (LMSYS)

## What it is

Crowdsourced blind comparison platform by LMSYS where humans chat with two anonymous LLMs simultaneously and vote for the better response.

## What problem it solves

Automated benchmarks may not capture real-world conversational quality; Chatbot Arena produces human-preference Elo rankings that correlate better with user satisfaction.
