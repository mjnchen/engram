# LangWatch

## What it is

Open-source LLM observability and evaluation platform for monitoring quality, cost, and latency of LLM applications in production.

## What problem it solves

Teams shipping LLM products need continuous visibility into output quality and cost without building custom logging pipelines from scratch.
