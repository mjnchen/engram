# Weights & Biases (LLMOps)

## What it is

Experiment tracking and LLMOps platform from Weights & Biases for logging, comparing, and monitoring LLM training runs and inference pipelines.

## What problem it solves

Training and fine-tuning runs produce large amounts of metrics and artifacts that are hard to track across experiments; W&B centralizes logging, comparison, and collaboration.
