# Opik

## What it is

Open-source LLM observability and evaluation platform from Comet ML for tracking experiments and production traces.

## What problem it solves

Connects offline experimentation (prompt testing, fine-tuning runs) with production tracing in a single platform so teams can correlate experiment results with live behavior.
