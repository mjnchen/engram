# Litgpt

## What it is

Litgpt is a high-performance LLM training and fine-tuning library by Lightning AI built on PyTorch Lightning with clean, hackable implementations of popular models.

## What problem it solves

Reference implementations of LLMs are often monolithic and hard to customize; Litgpt provides minimal, well-documented model code alongside practical training recipes for full fine-tuning, LoRA, and QLoRA.
