# CAMEL

## What it is

CAMEL is a multi-agent framework by CAMEL-AI for role-playing conversations between LLM agents, where agents with assigned roles collaborate or debate to solve complex tasks.

## What problem it solves

Single-agent LLMs struggle with tasks that benefit from diverse perspectives or division of labor; CAMEL coordinates multiple specialized agents through structured dialogue protocols.
