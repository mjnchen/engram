# Obsidian

## What it is

Markdown-based personal knowledge management application with a graph view, bidirectional linking, and a rich plugin ecosystem.

## What problem it solves

Notes in traditional apps are siloed; Obsidian's wikilink system and graph view surface connections between ideas, supporting a networked "second brain" approach to knowledge work.

## Notes

- Local-first: all data stored as plain Markdown files you own
- Sync and Publish are paid add-ons; core app is free
- 1000+ community plugins; available on desktop and mobile
