# Langfuse

## What it is

Open-source LLM engineering platform for tracing, evaluation, prompt management, and cost tracking in production.

## What problem it solves

LLM applications are hard to debug and monitor; Langfuse gives you full trace visibility, evaluation scores, and prompt versioning without locking you into a proprietary stack.
