# OLMo

## What it is

Fully open language model from the Allen Institute for AI (AI2) with training data (Dolma), training code, and model weights all publicly released.

## What problem it solves

Most open-weight models share weights but not training data or code, limiting reproducibility; OLMo enables the research community to study, audit, and replicate the full training pipeline.
