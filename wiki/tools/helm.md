# HELM

## What it is

Holistic Evaluation of Language Models — a broad benchmark suite from Stanford CRFM that measures LLM performance across accuracy, robustness, calibration, fairness, and efficiency.

## What problem it solves

Single-metric benchmarks miss important failure modes; HELM provides multi-dimensional scoring to give a fuller picture of model capability and risk.
