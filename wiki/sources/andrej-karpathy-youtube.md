# Andrej Karpathy YouTube Series

## Summary

Educational video series by Andrej Karpathy (ex-OpenAI, ex-Tesla) building neural networks and LLMs from scratch with exceptional pedagogical clarity. The series is the best single resource for developing deep mechanical intuition about how transformers actually work rather than how they are described abstractly.

## Key Points

- "Neural Networks: Zero to Hero" series progresses from backpropagation through micrograd → makemore → nanoGPT in a single coherent arc
- Each video builds directly on the previous — skipping ahead means missing foundational intuitions that later videos assume
- Tokenization video is the most complete public explanation of BPE, including why the vocabulary size matters for both efficiency and model quality
- GPT-2 reproduction video shows the full training run including hyperparameter tuning and loss curve analysis
