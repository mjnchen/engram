# Major LLMs + Data Availability Spreadsheet

## Summary

Community-maintained spreadsheet tracking openness of major LLMs across three dimensions: model weights, training data, and training code. The spreadsheet makes the "open source AI" debate concrete — most "open" models release weights but not data or code, which limits true reproducibility.

## Key Points

- Most models marketed as "open" release weights only — full openness (weights + data + code) is rare (GPT-NeoX, OLMo are exceptions)
- Weight availability alone enables fine-tuning and inference but not understanding training decisions or reproducing from scratch
- Training data openness is the most important dimension for safety auditing, as data drives model behavior more than architecture
- The spreadsheet helps identify which model claims to compare fairly — "open" means different things for different models
