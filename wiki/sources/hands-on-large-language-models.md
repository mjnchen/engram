# Hands-On Large Language Models

## Summary

Illustrated practical guide by Jay Alammar and Maarten Grootendorst covering LLMs from embeddings through RAG and deployment, with a strong emphasis on visual explanation. Best used as a companion to more technical resources — the visuals build accurate intuition where formulas alone don't.

## Key Points

- Semantic embeddings section explains why cosine similarity works for semantic search and how embedding spaces are structured
- Fine-tuning chapter distinguishes when fine-tuning is necessary vs. when RAG or prompting suffices
- RAG implementation section covers chunking strategies, retrieval quality evaluation, and re-ranking
- Production chapter addresses latency vs. quality trade-offs for different deployment scenarios
