# Awesome ChatGPT Prompts

## Summary

Community-curated collection of effective ChatGPT system prompts for persona-based use cases (act as a Linux terminal, act as a code reviewer, etc.). Less useful for understanding prompting theory, but valuable as a reference for the range of tasks LLMs can handle when given a specific framing.

## Key Points

- Persona prompts work by establishing context that biases the probability distribution — "you are an expert X" shifts what vocabulary and reasoning patterns are likely
- The most effective prompts in the collection are highly specific about format, constraints, and what not to do
- Many prompts use role assignment + constraint specification — two patterns that reliably improve instruction following
- The collection reveals that users discovered effective prompting patterns empirically before the research literature caught up
