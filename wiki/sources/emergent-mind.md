# Emergent Mind

## Summary

AI paper discovery service that surfaces trending arXiv papers with community discussion and Twitter/X context. Useful for staying current without reading every paper — the community signals identify which papers are worth reading in depth vs. skimming.

## Key Points

- Aggregates social signals (Twitter/X engagement, GitHub stars) to rank paper significance, not just recency
- Summaries are auto-generated but often accurate enough to decide whether a paper warrants full reading
- The "trending" view is particularly useful during conference periods (NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR) when hundreds of papers drop simultaneously
- Community comments sometimes contain important corrections or context not in the abstract
