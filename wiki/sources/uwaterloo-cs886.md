# UWaterloo CS 886 Foundation Models

## Summary

University of Waterloo graduate course on foundation models with lecture slides and paper lists covering architecture, training, adaptation, and applications. Valuable for covering domains beyond text — vision, multimodal, and scientific foundation models — not just language.

## Key Points

- Covers BERT, GPT, T5, and their successors with architectural comparisons, not just descriptions
- Multimodal section covers CLIP-style contrastive training and diffusion model foundations
- Adaptation section covers fine-tuning efficiency methods: LoRA, prefix tuning, prompt tuning, and adapter layers
- Scientific applications (protein language models, genomics) show how the foundation model paradigm generalizes beyond NLP
