# Stanford CS224n — NLP with Deep Learning

## Summary

Stanford's graduate NLP course. Lectures cover word vectors, RNNs, attention, transformers, pretraining, and fine-tuning. Lecture slides and videos are freely available. One of the best structured introductions to the transformer-era NLP stack.

## Key Points

- Covers the full arc from word2vec → attention → BERT → GPT
- Assignments build models from scratch in PyTorch
- Annual updates keep it current with the field
- Taught by Christopher Manning and others
